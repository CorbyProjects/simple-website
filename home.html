Projects in this site

Blog: I will document my adventures in web development across SharePoint, WordPress, and custom HTML/CSS/JavaScript-based sites. This is partially for fun stories, but mostly to keep a log of what I'm learning and monitor my progress.

Puzzle: I want a web-based puzzle game where a user can upload a photo and turn it into a puzzle. Ideally they should be able to upload many pictures, save their progress in multiple puzzles, etc. There will be no ads. Ever.

Bonsai Log: In pursuit of my gardening and bonsai hobby, there is a lot of information to keep track of in plant/bonsai development. This will provide a web-based app for gardeners to manage and store this information. That way I stop losing all of the papers.
